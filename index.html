Our Vision

Here at the CS Club, we act as a gateway for students to enter the Computer Science Community and Industry. We strive to educate, train, and guide students as they build towards a profession in CS Industry. We aren't just a club, we're an independent body where students learn together, help each other sharpen their skills and create their future in the industry.

FAQ Section

What does the CS Club do?

We help students in the field of Computer Science by:- Providing lectures and activities on the newests branches of Computer Science. Training them in the exciting sport of competitive programming. Introducing students to the Computer Science Industry by hosting Industrial Talks and Research Projects.

Is it only for the Computer Branch?

NO! All Branches are more than welcome to join the club and our events.
